Our Legacy

Welcome to Agrawal Jewellers, where tradition meets trust, and elegance meets excellence. We take pride in being one of the most loved and trusted jewellery destinations in Panchrasta, Sultanpur, since we began our journey in 1947.

At Agrawal Jewellers, we believe that jewellery is more than just an ornament—it's a reflection of emotions, memories, and timeless beauty. Since our inception, we have been committed to offering the finest quality gold and silver jewellery that celebrates tradition, style, and sophistication.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to provide authentic and exquisite jewellery to the people of Sultanpur and nearby regions, Agrawal Jewellers started as a small but passionate venture in 1947. From our humble beginnings, we have grown steadily with the love and trust of our customers, becoming a name that is now synonymous with quality, honesty, and personalized service.

What We Offer

  • Traditional Gold Necklaces, Earrings, Rings, and Bangles
  • Stylish Silver Payals, Chains, Anklets, and Religious Items
  • Customized bridal sets and festive collections
  • Hallmarked gold and 925 stamped silver articles

Our Promise

  • Personalized attention and honest recommendations
  • After-sales support and easy exchange policies
